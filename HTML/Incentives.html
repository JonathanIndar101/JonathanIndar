Our Incentives

The Ministry of Agriculture, Land and Fisheries incentive program is specifically designed to help small or commercially large farmers to successfully achieve their goals within the farming and fisheries division. These incentives serve as an indication of the Ministry encouraging our farmers to increase their crop production, the healthiness of their crop products, implementing the perseverance of the land and encouraging young youths into the agriculture sector. Incentives offered by our Ministry comprise of land development, machinery and vehicle equipment, crop maintenance chemicals/pesticides and security. With the Currency fluctuations of currency,Farmers should take this as their stepping stone to become sucessful in the agriculture industry and securing the food chain in Trinidad and Tobago.

Security

As farmers are prone to predators who try to steal their products or machinery. The ministry has put in place a few initiatives for farmers to protect their properties. These services are but limited to:

  • Security Camera Systems
  • Global Positioning System (GPS) installation in their equipment
  • Licensed Firearm
